Privacy Policy for DriveAlert Last updated: April 14, 2026 Introduction DriveAlert ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. Information We Collect DriveAlert stores the following information locally on your device: Notification Preferences: Your choices about which train lines and stations to monitor App Settings: Your preferences such as whether to enable notifications and alert thresholds How We Use Your Information The information stored on your device is used solely to: Remember your notification preferences between app sessions Provide personalised disruption alerts for your selected lines and stations Maintain your app settings and preferences Data Storage and Security All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not: Collect or transmit your personal information to our servers Share your data with third parties Track your location Collect any personally identifiable information Third-Party Services DriveAlert uses the following third-party services to provide real-time train information: Transport for London (TfL) API: Live status updates for London Underground and rail services National Rail (Darwin/OpenLDBWS): Live departure information for major London stations and airport expresses These services may collect anonymous usage data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies: TfL Privacy Policy National Rail Privacy Policy Push Notifications If you enable notifications, the app will send you alerts about train disruptions. You can disable notifications at any time through: The app's notification settings Your device's system settings Children's Privacy Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences. Data Deletion You can delete all locally stored data at any time by: Uninstalling the app from your device Clearing the app's data through your device settings Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

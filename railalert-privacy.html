Privacy Policy for RailAlert London

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Introduction

RailAlert London ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

RailAlert London collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Notification Preferences: Your choices about which train lines and stations to monitor for disruption alerts
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as whether to enable notifications and alert preferences

How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Remember your notification preferences between app sessions
  • Provide personalized disruption alerts for your selected lines and stations
  • Maintain your app settings and preferences

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not:

  • Collect or transmit your personal information to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your location
  • Collect any personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

RailAlert London uses the following third-party services to provide real-time train information:

  • Transport for London (TfL) API: To fetch live status updates for London Underground and rail services
  • Huxley2 API: To fetch National Rail departure information for major London stations

These services may collect anonymous usage data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • TfL Privacy Policy
  • Huxley2 Information

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app will send you alerts about train disruptions. You can disable notifications at any time through:

  • The app's notification settings
  • Your device's system settings

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

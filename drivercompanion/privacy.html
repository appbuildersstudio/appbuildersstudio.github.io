Driver Companion — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

1. Introduction

Driver Companion (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a companion app for professional ride-hailing drivers. The App provides real-time, on-device visual indicators based solely on information visible on your screen.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. By using Driver Companion, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Driver Companion does not collect any data.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any information of any kind. All processing happens locally on your device. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Ride request details, fares, distances, or times
  • Pickup or drop-off addresses or maps
  • Passenger names, numbers, or any rider data
  • Your earnings, mileage, or shift records (these stay on your device only)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Keystrokes or messages
  • Device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash reports
  • Any content from other apps on your device

Driver Companion makes no network calls and has no servers. Nothing leaves your phone.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

Any data you enter — such as shift earnings, mileage, notes, and your verdict thresholds — is stored only in the App’s local storage on your own device. It is never uploaded or shared. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted with it.

4. Permissions Used

Driver Companion requires two Android permissions to function:

Accessibility Service
Used exclusively to read the visible text of trip cards on your screen, so the App can calculate and display its real-time indicators. We do not read passwords, messages, personal data, or background activity.

Display Over Other Apps (Overlay)
Allows Driver Companion to display passive, non-interactive visual borders and an information card on your screen.

Driver Companion does not:

  • Interfere with third-party apps
  • Automate any actions
  • Modify any interface elements
  • Accept or reject rides
  • Block interaction with other apps

These permissions are essential to the App’s core function and are active only when monitoring is enabled.

5. Data Sharing

Because Driver Companion collects no data, there is nothing to share. We do not share any information with:

  • Ride-hailing platforms
  • Advertising partners
  • Analytics providers
  • Any third party

The App contains no advertising and no third-party tracking.

6. Children’s Privacy

Driver Companion is intended for professional drivers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have rights over your personal data. As Driver Companion collects and holds no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. Any data you create stays on your device and is fully under your control.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The most recent version will always be available at this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
